About Us

EACC is . . .

an Abbreviation for Ethiopian Agricultural consultation Company.we works on Developing Agricultural sector of the country. Our company joined the business back in 2012 GC, now we have a decade of experience which make as one of the best if not the best in the sector. We work with most of the biggest companies in agricultural sector. our clients are effectively utilizing our resources and services to improve their income from the farm land. our connections in the market helps a lot of farmers who were not able to get the market for their products. we are not only helping the business side of this but also we are enormously contributing to keeping environment clean and safe. our working policies are absolutely in alignment with the green legacy as our slogan described it "We are Here to Make Your Farm productive." If you choose to work with us feel free to check out more.
